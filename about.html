Heather Burch – CEO & Owner, inspiring leader and visionary 🌟

About Heather

Heather has a B.S. in Hospitality Management from the University of Arkansas and holds a Permaculture Design Certificate. To cap off her 20 years in the service industry, she's spent the last six years managing three million-dollar restaurants with an average of 75 employees. Her leadership, passion, and dedication inspire everyone around her.

Our Vision 🌄

Create a regenerative place to destress from daily life and reconnect with nature through short-term rentals and weekend retreats. A place nestled at the base of the mountains where by day you hit the trails and bask in nature. By night you enjoy the spa, feast on farm-to-table food, and enjoy a fire under the stars. A place to learn how to align with nature and find purpose through outdoor activities, farm-fresh food grown on site, and self-nourishing activities at the spa, hot tubs, and classes. This forms the foundation to a future regenerative agriculture food supply and a self-sustaining safe haven.
